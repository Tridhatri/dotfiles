
# Hacker Aesthetic: Terminal is Life 💀⚡

Normal Mode is not a feature.  
It is a way of life.  

GUI users click, drag, and scroll.  
I glide.  
I manipulate buffers, macros, and motions like a conductor directing an orchestra of ones and zeros.  

Every line of code I touch obeys.  
Every file I open bends to my will.  

Browsers? Disposable. IDEs? Training wheels for mortals.  
Vim? Eternal. Native. Uninstallable.  

If your workflow depends on a mouse, close this blog.  
You are not ready.
